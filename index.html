Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a capital cost estimating tool?

Yes — the BOQ Capital Cost Estimator is a free, browser-based AACE Class 4 estimating tool covering 214 equipment types across Process, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and Instrumentation disciplines. It uses Turton power-law correlations with CEPCI escalation, location factors for 19 countries, P/T correction, discipline-weighted indirect costs, sensitivity analysis, WBS tagging, offline mode and downloadable Quote List CSV export. No login required.

Are the calculators free to use?

Yes — all calculators are completely free, run in your browser and require no login, registration or subscription. They work on any device with a modern browser.

Which engineering standards are implemented?

ISO 5167 (orifice/flow), IEC 60534 (control valves), IAPWS-IF97 (steam properties), TEMA E/F/G/H/J/X (heat exchangers), ASME B31.3/VIII (piping/vessels), CTI ATC-105 (cooling towers), Darcy-Weisbach + Colebrook-White (pipe flow), ASME PTC 19.3 (thermowell analysis), API 12J (separators), Peng-Robinson & SRK EOS (gas properties).

Do calculators support both SI and Imperial units?

Yes. All process and fluid calculators support one-click switching between SI (metric) and US Customary (Imperial) unit systems. Results update instantly without re-entering data.

Can results be used for final engineering designs?

These calculators are suitable for preliminary engineering, feasibility studies, educational use and cross-checking. All results must be independently verified by a qualified registered engineer against applicable codes and standards before use in final designs.
